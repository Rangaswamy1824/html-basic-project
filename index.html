Mern stack

The Mern stack and the MEAN stack are both collections of popular JavaScript-based technologies used for web development. MEAN is an equivalent set of tools to MERN, simply replacing the React library and framework with another popular framework called Angular, also known as Angular.js. Both Angular and React are front-end technologies; the rest of the components stay the same. Both stacks have strengths and weaknesses. The best choice for your application will depend on your specific needs and preferences.
